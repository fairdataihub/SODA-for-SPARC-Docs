## Background

If you are experiencing an issue with SODA, have general feedback, or have a request about your data as per GDPR, you can contact the SODA team directly from within the SODA app.
This is done in the `Contact Us` page that is accessible from the sidebar. Here is how to navigate to the `Contact Us` page depending on where you are in the SODA app.

## Navigating to Contact Us page from the landing page

From the landing page, click the `Contact Us` tab in the sidebar. Then follow the on screen prompts to send your message to the SODA team.

## Navigating to Contact Us page from the Prepare Dataset Step-by-Step mode

Click `Save and Exit` to navigate to the landing page, then select the `Contact Us` tab in the sidebar. Then follow the on screen
prompts to send your message to the SODA team.

## Navigating to Contact Us page from the Upload Dataset mode

If you have not started an upload, click the `Contact Us` page in the sidebar. Then follow the on screen prompts to send your message to the SODA team.

## How to send the log files to the SODA team

Once you have the log files you can send them to us using the SODA contact form available (here)[/contact-us].
